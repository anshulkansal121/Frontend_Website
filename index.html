Our Facilities

The college meets the requirement of modern educational institution. Some of the major facilities are as follows:

LIBRARY

The college has more than 85,000 books, journals, periodicals and reference material on diverse fields.

SEMINAR HALL

The college has two modern state of art seminar rooms, fully air-conditioned, where various academic and cultural events are organized by the college.

CANTEEN

College has one of the largest canteens of Delhi University and can accommodate up to 200 people at single time. It offers variety of snacks, beverages and fresh fruits at reasonable rates.
