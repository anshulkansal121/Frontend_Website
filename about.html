ABOUT US

Pannalal Girdharlal Dayanand Anglo-Vedic (P.G.D.A.V) College is a constituent college of University of Delhi. Established in 1957, it offers various courses at the undergraduate, postgraduate level and open schooling. The College is situated in Nehru Nagar, near Lajpat Nagar, in New Delhi.

Infrastructure

The college meets the requirement of modern educational institution. Some of the major facilities are as follows:

Classrooms

College has smart classrooms with ceiling mounted projectors and roll down screens to facilitate teaching beyond the traditional style. College recently has added new academic block which constitutes 16 big classrooms and 12 small rooms. Library

The college has more than 85,000 books, journals, periodicals and reference material on diverse fields.

Computer Centre

The college has state of art computer network. The entire campus is wifi enabled, possesses well equipped computer lab with access to internet and DU library system which is available on all working days to students and teachers.

Seminar Rooms

The college has two modern state of art seminar rooms, fully air-conditioned, where various academic and cultural events are organized by the college.

Staff Room

A large and modern air-conditioned staff room for teaching faculty is available. It is one of the largest staff rooms of Delhi University and has a seating capacity of more than 150 people.

Departmental Rooms

The college has separate departmental rooms to facilitate research and academic activities of the faculty.

Power Backup

College has uninterrupted 24 X 7 power supply through its 400 KVA Electric Sub Station and 250 KW diesel generator.

Discipline

CCTVs have been installed throughout the college to ensure discipline and safety is maintained within the premises.

Lift

Lift facility is available for differently abled students and teachers.

Canteen

College has one of the largest canteens of Delhi University and can accommodate up to 200 people at single time. It offers variety of snacks, beverages and fresh fruits at reasonable rates.

RO Plant

The college has an RO Plant which provides RO water to its students, faculty as well as non teaching staff. Health of the staff and students is never compromised.

Bank

Central Bank of India branch is placed within the college premises, providing easy banking facilities to all staff and students.

Fire Fighting System

The college is equipped with a fire fighting system.

Parking

College has ample space to meet the parking requirements of the staff.
